fun City at Mercato is a complete family
entertainment that provides quality entertainment within
a safe and supervised environment; where the fun is.
Offering both mentally and physically stimulating activities,
Fun City is an affordable, entertaining and educational place to bring the family.
Visit the various attractions at Fun
City in Mercato such as redemption games,
rides and concession stands, as well as custom built play units
for developing gross motor skills. Fun & Learn – 40
activities where children’s talents are discovered and new hobbies are learnt.
Activities include a Musical area, Art & Creative Zone, Water & Sand
play area and a Reading Library full of children’s books.
Play Zone – 40 activities for children to play in, including a Ball Pool,
a Web Crawl, Slides, Tunnels and plenty more.
The structure stretches 5.4 meters high and 17
meters across giving enough room for 180 children to play in! Party House –
Choose from an array of fun and exciting themes
and packages, and celebrate a child’s birthday party
with warm and friendly party hostesses.
